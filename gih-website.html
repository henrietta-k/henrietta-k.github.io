Green Impact Hub: Youth Volunteer Website

  • HTML
  • CSS/ SCSS
  • JavaScript
  • GIH Website
  • Instagram

Green Impact Hub is an online platform led by a group of students passionate about environmental and social sustainability in Hong Kong. We focus on making sustainable changes through raising awareness of social-environmental issues, and launching workshops and events on environmental conservation and youth empowerment.

I made a prototype of a website that allows youth in Hong Kong to browse for sustainability opportunities. That way, it makes it easier for organizations to post volunteer opportunities and for youth to find and connect to them!
